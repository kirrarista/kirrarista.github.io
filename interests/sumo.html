Sumo

I once was looking for something random to watch at luch and it's been two years since I've stared following Grand Sumo. I obviously can't (and wouldn't be able to) practice sumo but following the life of the top division league has been a lot of fun.

Below is a short FAQ with quick answers about sumo.

Sumo FAQ

Question Answer
How many championships are there in a year? 6 in total: in Osaka, Nagoya, Fukuoka, and 3 in Tokyo.
What's the goal of sumo? Aside from a couple illegal moves, the rules are very simple. You lose if you touch the ground with anything but the soles of your feet or step outside the ring sooner than your opponent.
How long do bouts usually take? Shorter ones get desided in seconds, longer go up to a minute. On average, 20-30 seconds.
How can I follow sumo? NHK World Japan app posts highlight recordings every tournament with a half-a-day delay.
